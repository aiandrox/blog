```
fields @timestamp
| parse @message '"method":"*","path":"*","user_id":*,' as method, path, user_id
| fields @message, @logStream
| filter @logStream like /app/
| filter @timestamp >= 1671462000
| sort @timestamp desc
| limit 20
```
